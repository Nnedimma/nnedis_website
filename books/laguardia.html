LaGuardia

Future Nwafor Chukwuebuka, a pregnant Nigerian-American doctor, returns home from Nigeria to New York after successfully smuggling an illegal alien plant through LaGuardia’s customs checkpoint. This plant has a name, Letme Live, and its destiny is intertwined with Future and her unborn child. As Future and Letme face discrimination alongside African and shape-shifting alien immigrants, Future will undergo more than a few changes as she hides a dark secret.
