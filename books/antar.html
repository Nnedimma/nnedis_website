Antar: The Black Knight

Written by Nnedi Okorafor, illustrated by Eric Battle

Honor through perseverance. Legacy through diversity

IDW is proud to present the epic story of one of history’s greatest warriors and finest poets: Antar the Black Knight. A despised camel driver born of an African slave mother and an Arab Noble father, Antar proves that heroes are made by embracing who we are and dreaming about what we can become.
